The Psychology Behind Our Approach

We leverage cognitive science to create ADHD-friendly learning experiences

Dopamine Engagement

Our system triggers dopamine release through frequent small achievements, progress tracking, and surprise rewards to maintain motivation.

Visual Anchoring

Color-coded information, icons, and diagrams create visual anchors that help with information retention and recall.

Interactive Encoding

Active participation through quizzes and puzzles creates stronger memory traces than passive reading.

Time Perception

Progress indicators and timers make abstract study time concrete, reducing time-related anxiety.

Color Psychology

Strategic use of high-contrast colors draws attention to key concepts without overwhelming the senses.

Reduced Cognitive Load

Information is chunked and presented with minimal distractions to prevent cognitive overload.
